Buhloh.buzz Tasting Map No. 042

Ezra Brooks Kentucky Straight Bourbon

"The Pretender" — Jackson Browne

Ezra Brooks / Lux Row Distillers · Kentucky Straight Bourbon · 90 Proof · 12.5 Years · The Hambletonian, Heritage China Series

Nose Palate Finish
N

Nose

Roasted corn on the cob with butter, followed by black walnut, varnished antique oak, marzipan, and then vanilla cream. Ends with just a whisper of ethanol.

  • Corn
  • Oily
  • Walnut
  • Oak
  • Almond
  • Vanilla
  • Creamy
  • Overproof
P

Palate

A long, slow pull of caramel, vanilla, nutmeg, and clove. Structured oak and the start of some lightly drying tannins. Baking spices return on the back palate.

  • Caramel
  • Vanilla
  • Nutmeg
  • Clove
  • Oak
  • Astringent
  • Cinnamon
F

Finish

Cinnamon and clove dissolve into caramel, clotted cream, and then a gently Kentucky hug. Low-proof entry into the barrel melds these flavors together in rolling waves.

  • Cinnamon
  • Clove
  • Caramel
  • Creamy
